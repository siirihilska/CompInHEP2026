In the GEANT4 example basic/B4/B4a the simulated detector is the following:
- It is a calorimeter (side length 10 cm), with absorbers and gaps alternating. Absorbers are 1 cm thick lead plates by default, whereas the gaps are 0.5 cm thick liquid argon layers. 

Additional note:
I included macro files that can be used to run the desired simulations. However, there seems to be some kind of mismatch problem with OGL and exporting the pictures, because the detector doesn't show in the produced pictures. That's why I worked in interactive mode and took screenshots. You can also see the exported pictures (.eps files) without the detector in my returned answers for the first part of the exercise using lead plates as absorber.
